Alright I Will Tell My Story

I'm 36 and I'm currently in a situation that I never planned. in 2000 I was studying in England and ended up being hospitalized for Drug Induced Paranoia and then came back to the states and found my folks took it up as a parenting technique when I would stay up for 36 hours at a time studying web design or reading online they would hospitalize me. I ended up being determined to have a permanent disability known as Bipolar Disorder Type 1 and I currently live in an apartment.

I graduated from University of Cincinnati in 2010 with a degree in English Literature. I also spent a year with the Public Allies and lost over 150 pound in a year which was brought on by medications.

God smote me.

I have been smited by God.

God legit smited me.

I wouldn't say I am stupid. I will point out I was not diagnosed with being stupid and I have had case managers say that they don't really think there is anything wrong with me in say New York City where I would probably get along better and I'm not moving because I don't really care for NYC and have been there enough times to know things like that city sleeps just like mine does.

I just last week wrote 15 literary agents and am working my way through a Writer's Market Guide to Literary agents because I have enough writing projects started and finished that I could use an agent to help me with my publishing career.

I recently had a book of mine read by a colleague in london that thinks the ending needs changed so I am in the process of working on that.

I live on social security and now live an author's life. I have a guaranteed check for life. I will never be homeless because of my quote unquote permanent condition. My student loans were forgiven and all that keeps me from having a job is applying which I did today and continually do on a almost bi weekly basis apply for work on Career Builder and today I applied at McDonalds.

I note that the vast majority of the authors I looked up to and look up to have and had day jobs.

I know some things. In high school I was the valedictorian of my class of 400 and now unless you really talked to me you wouldn't know how intelligent I was.

The book my friend read had him ecstatic he loved what he read and just requests that the story become more real instead of copping out with a dream sequence at the end.

I recently lost a good friend because she told me she attempted to kill herself and I told her to check herself into a hospital because she knows the drill with her PHD in psychiatry. She screamed at me and ended our friendship and I proceeded to let her husband know on facebook and I called her place of work and notified them as well and the magazine she was working on a BDSM project on. We are no longer friends and I'm left with more intelligence maybe about how people really feel about psychiatric hospitalization.

I'm normal quite normal most of the time sitting and reading online or writing.

I've quite been told I am the most intelligent client Talbert House has ever seen. It's quite possible I have been misdiagnosed and the fights I got into with my mother were seen as mental illness when I lived with them while I was studying.

I don't know how hard it is to make it in literature because I have placed pieces of my writing online. I have placed works on Amazon. I have been published in Thought Catalog and Alt Ohio and the Cincinnati New Record at Uc.

I would say I am more interesting than most people just because how often can someone claim God smited them.

I have tried learning computer software engineering but I just can't be fucked to do it. I study HTML and CSS and I don't know why I need more than that. I think a lot of web design it kitsch and lacking leadership and I think my designs stand out because they are always based on something and never really relying on gimmicks. I think web design is simple. Display the Content in an Organized fashion. I think a lot of web designers showcase flash and wow and I THINK if people really thought about it when you are asking a web designer to do a website for you you are letting the web designer know that you are prepared to do a lot of content generation. Web design to me is sort of like hiring someone to be your boss for three months. Web design is nothing without content and I see a lot of web designers developing web sites without thought as to the content. I think there are web designers that itching to use their CSS3 and Javascript skills and for the clients I have had that is not even been necessary or been discussed as pertinent to the job.

I do a lot. It's a nice life living with a permanent mental disability that does not impair judgement or intelligence. Not really sure why this is a permanent disability but I get a free paycheck and ostensibly a free apartment and it's not a bad life.

I am seeking work and I would say I went through a major life change. I spent a little under 2 years in state and city hospitals for Bipolar Type 1 and I now get a regular injection of Invega Sustena and and am ordered by the courts to basically do whatever they tell me to do.

I learned that in a way you're an enemy of the state if you have a mental illness. I'm not mentally ill outside of Hamilton County so when or rather IF I move i'll be quitting my medications or I might see a private psychiatrist to get some pills and avoid the injection.

I'm in a complicated situation and i'm 36 and I used to cry out about how unfair this is.

Now I just believe that God smites people.
